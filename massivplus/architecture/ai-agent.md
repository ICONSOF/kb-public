# AI agent — AP3

## Purpose

The AI guidance layer sits on top of the data space infrastructure. Its primary audience is SME users (Erik and Nisse personas) who lack sustainability expertise and dedicated IT resources.

## Core functions

- **Onboarding guidance** — help a new user understand what data to collect and why
- **Data structuring** — transform raw inputs (energy invoices, ERP exports, PDF bills) into CCDM-structured data
- **Quality checks** — flag anomalies, gaps, or values that look wrong
- **Analysis and explanation** — help users understand their numbers and what they mean
- **Action recommendations** — what should this company prioritise to reduce its footprint?

## Target outcome

15-minute onboarding for a small supplier (Nisse persona) with no IT department.
