# KPIs and success metrics

Target outcomes from the Vinnova project application.

## Operational targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| SME onboarding time | 15 minutes |
| Reduction in time from data collection to analysis | 90% shorter lead time |
| Reduction in time/cost for establishing and maintaining Scope 3 data | 80% reduction |

## Data quality targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Scope 3 data quality | Based on real supplier data (replacing industry averages) for the first time |
| Data freshness | Quarterly updates (vs annual today) |

## Scale targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Minimum Viable Data Space | 2 large companies + 3 SMEs (TRL 7) |
| Scale-up during project period | 50+ SMEs |
| Governance model | TRL 8 (governance and incentive model validated) |

## Emission reduction targets

| Timeframe | Target |
|---|---|
| Initial years | 30–50% annual emission reductions |
| Subsequent years | 10–20% annual |
| 5–8 year horizon | 75% total reduction |

---

*These are the targets from the grant application. As the pilot progresses, add actual measured results alongside the targets.*
