# Onboarding — new team member

Welcome. This guide helps you get oriented quickly.

## Start here

1. **Company context** — read `company/overview.md` to understand what Icons Of does and where we focus
2. **The project** — read `massivplus/overview.md` for context on MASSIV+, then `massivplus/work-packages.md` to understand what we're responsible for
3. **The users** — read `massivplus/personas.md`. These four personas are central to everything we build
4. **The standards** — skim `standards/ghg-protocol.md` and `standards/csrd-overview.md` if you're not already familiar with sustainability reporting basics
