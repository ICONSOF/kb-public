# Icons Of — Company overview

## What we are

Icons Of is a native AI company turning sustainability data into real business value. Based in Onsala (Kungsbacka), Sweden. We combine expertise in data science, AI, business applications, and sustainability to help organisations navigate EU sustainability reporting requirements and turn ESG data into measurable business impact.

**Website:** iconsof.se | **Org nr:** 559433-3592

## What we do

### Forward Deployment Engineering

AI-driven solutions developed and implemented close to the user and the business. Combines advanced analytics with practical impact, integrating directly into operations. From identifying valuable data to deploying AI agents that guide decisions.

### Climate Screening & Spend-Accounting

AI-driven service for calculating environmental impact based on financial data. Identifies which accounts contribute the most CO2 emissions. Used as a cost-effective way to include lower-priority activities in carbon accounting reports. Fully aligned with CSRD and GHG Protocol.

Key use case: the Peab project — AI-augmented Scope 3 analysis across thousands of accounts and transaction volumes.

### Consulting Services

Tailored sustainability data consulting:

- ESG data readiness assessment and diagnostics
- Data collection, structuring, and governance
- Implementation of Microsoft Sustainability Manager (MSM)
- CSRD/ESRS and VSME compliance support
- Data governance and alignment of strategy, reporting, and technology

We bridge the gap between sustainability expertise and modern technology. Sustainability accounting is as complex as financial accounting — it requires purpose-built systems, not spreadsheets.

### The Cards (SaaS platform)

AI-assisted platform at thecards.eu. Helps organisations operationalise CSRD and VSME requirements.

- Each ESRS/VSME disclosure is translated into actionable "cards"
- Teams assign ownership, track progress, and align efforts per disclosure
- Built to meet European reporting standards
- Designed to unlock business value beyond compliance

### The Big Picture

AI-powered workflows for double materiality assessment:

- Identify key sustainability topics
- Map value chains
- Understand risks and opportunities across environmental, social, and governance areas

Results connect directly to The Cards for reporting and collaboration.

### MASSIV+ (data infrastructure for supply chain climate data)

Icons Of is the technical project lead for the MASSIV+ Vinnova project. See `massivplus/overview.md` for full context.

We lead three of six work packages:
- **AP2** — Data model and infrastructure
- **AP3** — AI-based guidance
- **AP4** — Pilot and validation
